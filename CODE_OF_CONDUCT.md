# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this repository to be a respectful, professional place for collaboration. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to participate in good faith and treat one another with respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but be honest and specific about problems.
- Welcome questions from people who are new to the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject participation, or block access when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report through the repository maintainers or GitHub moderation channels rather than escalating in public threads.
